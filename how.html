How It Works

  • Donate Leftover Food: Simply sign up, log your leftovers, and we take care of the rest. Our volunteers ensure safe and timely pickup from your doorstep.
  • Earn Points: Each donation is rewarded with points you can redeem for merchandise, discount coupons,
  • Our Trusted Network: We collaborate with NGOs, shelters, restaurants, and local businesses to ensure the food reaches those in need efficiently.
  • Impact Tracking: View your contribution stats and see how many lives you've helped impact through your dashboard.
  • Simple & Safe: We follow food safety protocols and make the entire process smooth and secure for both donors and recipients.
