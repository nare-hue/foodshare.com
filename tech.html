Tech that we use:

Food Share leverages cutting-edge technology to ensure efficient and equitable food distribution. Cold storage facilities maintain food quality, while drones and wheeled robots swiftly deliver meals to those in need. AI algorithms precisely calculate daily food requirements, optimizing resource allocation. Robotic systems meticulously prepare meals and affix nutrition labels, guaranteeing consistent quality and transparency for recipients.
